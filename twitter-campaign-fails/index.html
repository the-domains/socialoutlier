Twitter Campaign Case Study:


#SummerinSyria

On a similar (but much sadder) note, during
the summer
of 2015
Syria’s state news


agency SANA launched a

Twitter campaign

called#SummerinSyria. The hope, we
can


only assume, was an
attempt to show the
world that

Syria has some wonderful



qualities.





SANA probably imagined that Syrian
visitors and natives
would
take the
opportunity
to
share happy images of


families picnicking
in the park.





Instead, people in Syria used the hashtag
ironically to
post photos
of war,

destruction,
illness and devastation,


ultimately
undermining the intention of the

campaign
and
enlightening the
world to just
how dangerous the

situation in Syria
has
become.
